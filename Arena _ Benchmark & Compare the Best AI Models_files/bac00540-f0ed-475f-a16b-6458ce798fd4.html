About TURIHO

Bridging Distance with Trust & Care

TURIHO Ltd is a professional family care and representation service for the Burundian diaspora. Founded in 2024 with headquarters in Bujumbura and management in Stockholm, we act as your trusted physical presence on the ground in Burundi.

🛡️

Trusted & Verified Agents

Every agent is thoroughly vetted, trained, and monitored to ensure the highest standards of care and professionalism.

📡

Real-Time Communication

Stay connected with photo updates, reports, and direct communication — all from your phone, wherever you are.

❤️

Family-First Approach

We treat every family like our own. Your loved ones' wellbeing is our mission, not just a service.

How It Works

Simple Steps to Peace of Mind

Getting started with TURIHO is easy. From sign-up to your first service delivery, we keep things transparent and straightforward.

1

Sign Up

Create your account through WhatsApp or our web platform. Tell us about your family and what you need.

2

Get Matched

We assign a trusted, verified agent in your family's area who understands local needs and speaks your language.

3

Request Services

Request visits, deliveries, monitoring, or any service you need. Everything is coordinated through your phone.

4

Stay Connected

Receive real-time updates, photos, and reports. Know your family is cared for — anytime, from anywhere.
